# User Workflow After All Tasks Are Complete

1. Open SplitShot and go to the Project pane.
2. Click `Connect PractiScore`.
3. Complete the PractiScore login or challenge in the visible PractiScore browser window.
4. Wait until SplitShot shows that the session is ready.
5. Choose one remote match from the available match list.
6. Click `Import Selected Match`.
7. Let SplitShot download the selected match CSV or TXT file, HTML page, and summary snapshot.
8. Review the local `Match type`, `Stage #`, `Competitor name`, and `Place` controls after import.
9. Continue your normal workflow in Splits, Score, Review, Metrics, Overlay, and Export.
10. If you want a different remote match, choose it and import it again.
11. If your session expires later, click `Connect PractiScore` again and sign in.
12. If remote import is unavailable for any reason, use `Select PractiScore File` as the fallback path.
13. Export your final video once imported data and timing look correct.
