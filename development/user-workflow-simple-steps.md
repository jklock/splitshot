# User Workflow After All Tasks Are Complete

1. Open SplitShot and go to the Project pane.
2. Click Connect PractiScore.
3. Complete the PractiScore login/challenge when prompted.
4. Wait until SplitShot shows that PractiScore is connected.
5. Click Sync PractiScore.
6. Let SplitShot automatically find and import your match results.
7. Review the imported match type, stage, competitor, and place fields.
8. Continue your normal workflow in Splits, Score, Review, Metrics, Overlay, and Export.
9. If sync reports partial failures, read the error summary and re-run sync if needed.
10. If your session expires later, click Connect PractiScore again and sign in.
11. If sync is unavailable for any reason, use Select PractiScore File as fallback.
12. Export your final video once imported data and timing look correct.
