# Code of Conduct

This project expects professional, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and direct.
- Focus on the code, the design, and the issue at hand.
- Respect different levels of experience and different working styles.
- Give feedback that helps another person move the work forward.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or abusive language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or review threads.
- Personal attacks or attempts to intimidate contributors.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
